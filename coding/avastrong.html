LEGS!!!

Ava's favorite workout!

  • 1-2 mile run or 30 minutes on Stair Master
  • 3 sets 8-12 Reps: Dumbell RDL's
  • 3 sets 6-12 Reps(Heavy Weight): Smith Machine Hip Thrust.
  • 3 sets 10-12 Reps per leg: Dumbell Step Ups."Those really do the job"
  • 3 sets 8-12 Reps: Leg Press(Single or Double leg)
  • 3 sets 10-20 Reps: Hyper-extensions Glute focused (Back Rounded)
  • Abs and Stretching

Arms and Back!!!

Second to Legs

  • 1-2 mile run or 30 minutes on Stair Master
  • 3 sets 6-10 Reps: Machine Press
  • 3 sets 8-12 Reps: Dumbell Row
  • 3 sets 8-12 Reps: Underhand Row
  • 3 sets 8-12 Reps: Lat Pull-Downs
  • 3 sets 8-12 Reps: Tricep Extensions
  • 3 sets Until Failure: Assisted Pullups
  • Abs and Stretching

Diet

Important!!

  • Maintain a high protein intake
  • Focus on keeping fatty foods to a minimum
  • Use supplements such as Protein Powder and Creatine
  • Stay consistent with the amount of calories you intake
Creatine is one of the most researched supplements in the world, and is proven to have no negatives. Creatine helps retain water retention in muscles, which allows you to get a few extra reps at the end of your set.
